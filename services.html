The Orange Initiative

The Orange Purse

We stand for social justice for the girls and women in societ. We believe that investing in girls' education transform communities, countries & the entire world. We will start giving sanitary towels to young girls in schools this year. To reduce unemployment, we are also going to equipt, inform & sponsor local communities with the equipment they need to produce their own washable pad.

The Orange Bank

Learning to save is not just an essential money habit, saving teaches discipline and delayed gratification. We will Start training primary school learners about savings. An STC "orange piggy banks" which they will use to save money will be given to them, then later in the year they will have to figure out how much they were able to save.

The Orange Relief

We can work with communities and major retailers to facilitate disaster relief by providing essential supplies such as food, clothing, and medical aid. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to ensure that those affected by natural disasters receive timely assistance and resources to rebuild their lives. Our network of partners allows us to mobilize quickly and efficiently in times of crisis.
