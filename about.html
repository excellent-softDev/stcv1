Welcome to Start The Conversation™

Start The Conversation™ ("STC") is a dedicated team of individuals driven by a shared vision of creating positive change in our community and beyond. Our organisation was founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to essential resources, opportunities, and a better quality of life.

South Africans struggle with managing their finances, debts, and saving for the future. Limited financial knowledge often leads to poor financial decisions and perpetuates the cycle of poverty. Start The Conversation™ appointed a braille administrator and will be offering 10 subjects in braille to service the blind community

What role does Start The Conversation™ (“STC”) play in promoting financial literacy?

  • Empower individuals financial growth.
  • Create awareness to your financial situation.
  • Interact engagement on life experiences And basic money management.

Through our collective efforts, we aim to create Consumer Awareness to uplift the financial well-being of our communities. STC™ aims to achieve this by providing free in-person, virtual and 3D (digital training) with our YouTube Channel "STC™ with Izaka (@stc101)" using topical subjects on identified knowledge gaps that will lead to a brighter future for all.

All staff are qualified financial facilitators in line with the Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA) requirements.

STC™ Values

Our Core Values

Empowerment: At the heart of everything we do is the belief that financial education is fundamental to empowering individuals. By providing people with the knowledge and tools to make informed financial decisions, we enable them to take control of their financial futures, improve their personal well-being, and contribute to the economic growth of their communities.

Inclusivity: We recognize the diverse needs of individuals across different demographics, and our mission is to make financial education accessible to all. We design our resources and programs to be relevant and helpful for people from all walks of life, whether they’re from low-income households or affluent communities, urban or rural areas, or varied cultural backgrounds. Everyone deserves the opportunity to enhance their financial literacy.

Sustainability: Financial education isn’t just about short-term success; it’s about creating lasting, generational change. We aim to inspire and equip individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to make sound financial decisions that benefit not just themselves, but also the wider community. By promoting sustainable financial practices, we’re helping to ensure that future generations inherit a more financially stable world.

Integrity: Trust is the foundation of any successful educational initiative, and we are committed to maintaining transparency and honesty in all our interactions. We ensure that all our content is clear, factual, and designed to build credibility with our audience. Our goal is to cultivate an environment where learners feel confident in the accuracy and fairness of the financial information provided.
